The Best Series According to Saif

My Top Series of all Time

Kurulus Osman

The epic story of Osman Gazi, founder of Ottoman Empire, from his early years to his death, based on the novel by Tarik Bugra.

Mehmetçik Kut'ül Amare

It is about the Siege of Kut al-Amara, which the Ottoman Empire ended with a decisive victory on the Iraqi Front during World War I, against Britain.

Muhtesem Yüzyil

From 1520, follows Suleiman the Magnificent and his relatives from his great conquests to the "Battle of Szigeth".
